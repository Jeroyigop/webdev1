Cheese-flavored hexagon-shaped potato crisps

Piattos are thin, hexagon-shaped potato crisps made from dehydrated potatoes, potato starch, and palm oil, with a cheese powder blend of whey, cheddar cheese, and buttermilk.

Fun Fact

Piattos are designed with a hexagonal shape that is meant to provide a better, more satisfying "crunch" compared to regular round potato chips.

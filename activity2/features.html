Core Features

Piattos deliver a crunchy cheese snack experience with a fun, hexagonal shape.

  • Thin, hexagon-shaped crisps for a satisfying crunch.
  • Cheesy flavor from a whey, cheddar, and buttermilk powder mix.
  • Made from dehydrated potatoes and potato starch for a crispy texture.
